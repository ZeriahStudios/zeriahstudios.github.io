Zeriah Studios

Zeriah Studios is the gaming and entertainment arm of Zeriah Group. Our focus is on capturing the timeless fun factor that made games of the 80s and 90s so memorable.

Rather than chasing only visuals, we build lighthearted experiences designed for laughter, joy, and family bonding — moments that bring people closer together.

In a world where social media often creates distance, our intention is simple: to create games that make people smile and share time with one another.
